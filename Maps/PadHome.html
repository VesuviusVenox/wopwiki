PadHome

PadHome is a PadMap by ENTE that was never updated for the PadMod, nor newer versions of WoP. It's supposedly PadMan's Home.

Powerups

The following is a list of powerups that can be found in this map:
2 Shard Packs
1 PadShield
1 Revival
1 Visionless
1 PadPower

Weapons

The following is a list of weapons that can be found in this map:
1 Pumper
1 Balloony
1 Betty
1 Boaster
1 Splasher
1 Bubble G.

Gametypes

This map supports the following Gametypes:
Free For All
Team Deathmatch
Tournament

Secrets

In the starting room, if the player goes behind the throne, there will be a large button. Upon pressing it, a door behind them will open, revealing a bunch of toilets and a bath, along with the boaster and some ammo.
The revival, although isolated, is quite easy to obtain. Simply push the button in front of the gate and it will open. If the gate closes while a player is still inside, they won't die but anyone can open the gate from the outside. The player also has the option to open the gate from the inside as well.
