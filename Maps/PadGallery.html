PadGallery

PadGallery was a PadMap by ENTE. It had two versions: Main version and "DeLuxe" version. There was also a third small version with erotical art called "XXX Version", however the only changes were the pictures in the gallery.

Powerups

The following is a list of powerups that can be found in this map:
2 Shard Packs
1 PadShield
1 Revival
1 Visionless
1 PadPower

Weapons

The following is a list of weapons that can be found in this map:
1 Nipper
2 Pumpers
1 Balloony
1 Betty
1 Boaster
1 Splasher
1 Bubble G.
1 Imperius

Gametypes

This map supports the following Gametypes:
Free For All
Team Deathmatch
Tournament

Secrets

In order to get the Imperius, the player must enter the bathrooms and use the pumper to boost themselves up to one of the stalls, which will teleport them to the hidden room containing the gun.
If the player uses the pumper to jump over the reception desk, they can push a button to open a secret door on the floor and get the Visionless. (shown in the video below)
