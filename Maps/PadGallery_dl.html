PadGallery DeLuxe

PadGallery DeLuxe (Or PadGallery DL for short) is the updated version of the original map. This map is also used as the old PadGallery in the PadMod port and PadPack, however it has powerups and weapons moved and health stations. The map also has better movement between areas as different rooms are now connected, unlike the previous version.

Powerups

The following is a list of powerups that can be found in this map:
1 Shard Pack
1 PadShield
1 Climber
1 Revival
1 Visionless
1 PadPower

Weapons

The following is a list of weapons that can be found in this map:
2 Pumpers
1 Balloony
2 Bettys
1 Boaster
1 Splasher
2 Bubble Gs

Gametypes

This map supports the following Gametypes:
Free For All
Team Deathmatch
Tournament

Secrets

The Imperius in this map is replaced with the Visionless powerup. Accessing it is the same as it was in the original version. (Pumper jumping into a stall in the bathrooms)
If the player uses the pumper to jump over the reception desk, they can push a button to open a secret door on the floor and get the Revival. (shown in the video below)
Inside the new storage room, there are stacked crates where the PadShield is. If the player uses the pumper to climb them to the top crate, they can jump inside and will be teleported to the top of a ceiling lamp in the main foyer and pick up the Climber.

Music

PadGallery DL features music from the original PadGallery, which is in Dieselkopf's latest in-game album called " Industrial Puppet " (WoP 1.6)
