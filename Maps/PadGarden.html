PadGarden

PadGarden was a PadMap by ENTE which is updated for the latest version of WoP. PadGarden went through the most changes throughout the years out of all the PadMaps. There is also a secondary PadMap version of PadGarden which is more like the current version.

Powerups

The following is a list of powerups that can be found in this map:
2 Shard Packs
1 PadShield
1 Revival
1 Visionless
1 PadPower

Weapons

The following is a list of weapons that can be found in this map:
2 Pumpers
1 Balloony
1 Betty
1 Boaster
3 Splashers
1 Bubble G.
1 Imperius

Gametypes

This map supports the following Gametypes:
Free For All
Team Deathmatch
Tournament

Secrets

If the player goes all the way up to the top of the book rack next to the front door, they will find a portal leading to the room containing the Imperius of this map. The alternate route to the imp room is jumping in the pool and pushing the obvious button that is on a rock in the center of the pond. Upon doing so, the same door used to exit the room is opened, however this method is not very safe as you are very vulnerable while swimming around in the water.
Similar to newer iterations of the map, the radio at the windowsill has a button that can be pushed by the player's model, which in return will play music. The music played is "Mr. Sandman" from the Chordettes.
