PC Gamer Presents: ENTE'S PadShop

ENTE's PadShop was a PadMap for Quake 3 with a PC Gamer theme. It's one of ENTE's most successful and popular PadMaps. This map was later added to the PadMod with lots of changes, but removed from WoP 1.0. The map was reborn in WoP 1.5, with completely different graphics to fit the game. In WoP, PadShop can work perfectly fine just like every other Q3 map, however it's recommended to play it via the PadMod port, since it has mostly Q3 Textures. In regular WoP, most textures would be missing.

Secrets

This map has numerous secret rooms. Here's a list of all of them and how to access each one (Also showcased in the video below)
Doom Room: Contains a PadShield, Betty and some ammo packs. To access it, go to the back of the map where a doom poster is and jump into it.
PC Room: Contains a bunch of ammo, a shard pack and PadPower. To access it, simply jump into the monitor of the desk PC.
Space: Contains a PadShield, a bunch of ammo and the Imperius. To access it, jump into the rocket and push the button for liftoff. NOTE: This is a very slow process, as the rocket has to slowly move out of the map. You will miss out on a lot of frags this way, so don't do it unless you are good enough to survive and use the Imperius at least once.
Inside the cupboard: Contains a bunch of ammo, a shard pack, a Pumper and Revival. To access it, go to the top and jump down to the open cupboard and into the small hole.

Music

PadShop featured music by Dieselkopf, however, the track was never added to the nowadays PadRock'n'Roll in-game playlist. (Unlike PadSpace and PadGallery). There is a way to add the track that loops in the PadShop to your regular WoP playlist. You first need to find the PadShop music file inside the map's .pk3 file. After exporting it, use a program like Audacity to convert the .wav file into a .ogg file, name it "10_PadShop.ogg" for WoP 1.2, or "11_PadShop.ogg" for WoP 1.6. Then go to your WoP directory, open the "wop" folder and open the "wop_004.pk3" archive. go to "wopmusic" then "Dieselkopf1" and transfer the PadShop.ogg file into the archive. Then open your game and you should have the same track in your PadRock'n'Roll playlist as the last track.
