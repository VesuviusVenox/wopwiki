PadPool

PadPool is a PadMap by ENTE for Quake 3. It is one of the 9 PadMaps that weren't ported and updated for the latest versions of WoP. It was also made for the "Enten Clan" (Presumably ENTE's Clan in Q3)

Powerups

The following is a list of powerups that can be found in this map:
1 Shard Pack
1 PadShield
1 Revival
1 Visionless
1 PadPower

Weapons

The following is a list of weapons that can be found in this map:
1 Pumpers
1 Balloony
2 Bettys
1 Boaster
1 Splasher
1 Bubble G.
1 Imperius

Gametypes

This map supports the following Gametypes:
Free For All
Team Deathmatch
Tournament

Secrets

If the player jumps into the crack in the wall next to the boaster, Richard Wagner's Ride of the Valkyries will begin playing in the background for about a minute or so.
Similarly, if the player climbs to the top of the big rock formation, the same sound will play.
One of the buttons on the power box (where the Bubble G. is located) can be activated (it is indicated with a PadMan logo above it). Upon doing so, a betty shot will fire where the Imperius is, killing anyone trying to get it.
In order to get the Imperius in this map, one must sacrfice some of their health. Simply jump in the pond and swim to the very bottom. Be careful though, as you are guaranteed taking at least 10 points of damage when resurfacing. You also run the risk of being shot by the betty blast if someone else gets the idea to activate the secret button, and you're left vulnerable to opposing attacks from behind whilst swimming.
This is the first padmap to feautre Imperius ammo. The only way to get it however, is by doing tricky wall jumps with the Betty + PadPower combo. It takes a lot of practice and it is considered a waste of the powerup by most, especially since the second lamp (showcased in the video below) is very difficult to reach.
