PadKitchen

PadKitchen is a PadMap by ENTE that has been updated for all the different versions of WoP throughout the years. The first version of PadKitchen is very different from the nowadays versions. For example, the microwave is not a killroom and the climber is located there, the way to the imperius actually leads to the microwave etc.

Powerups

The following is a list of powerups that can be found in this map:
2 Shard Packs
1 PadShield
1 Climber
1 Revival
1 Visionless
1 PadPower

Weapons

The following is a list of weapons that can be found in this map:
2 Pumpers
1 Balloony
2 Bettys
1 Boaster
2 Splashers
2 Bubble Gs
1 Imperius

Gametypes

This map supports the following Gametypes:
Free For All
Team Deathmatch
Tournament

Secrets

If the player jumps into the large open hole in the underground section of the map, they will be taken to the microwave. When pushing the button on the wall, a door on the opposide side will open, revealing the Climber in this map.
From the outside of the microwave, a button on it can be shot in order to turn the machine on, killing any player(s) that are inside instantly, thus making the Climber very risky and difficult to get. (Though it doesn't even do anything so you shouldn't bother with it)
The light switch, similarly to newer versions of the map, can be shot, but it doesn't appear to have any effect on the enviroment, unlike newer versions of the map.
There's a radio near the sink, hanging from the cupboard, which, if shot, will play a tune, similar to modern releases of the map.
If a player reaches the Imperius using the Betty + PadPower jump combo, an announcer will yell "YEEEEES" in an excited tone. This can be heard by all players all around the map, signalling them that someone has the gun. This is also one of the few maps where instead of having ammo scattered across the map, the actual gun comes loaded with 5 shots right off the bat
