Boaster

"This is a really strange weapon. Its jet, curved like a water jet, causes a lot of damage. Besides the damage, the jet will make the floor very slippery. It is not easy to move on this floor, so you will be an easy target if you walk on it." — World of Padman Manual
In World of Padman, the Boaster fires a powerful, curved jet of water at a target. It is most effective from point-blank to medium range. The Boaster eats up ammo at lightning speed, but can quickly do damage to a target.

Strategies

•The Boaster can be used for speed boosting as it can create a slippery surface if fired towards the ground, giving the player an advantage to boost speed without jumping, provided if the player knows how to strafe jump.

Trivia

•The Boaster is based off of the Lightning Gun from Quake 3 Arena.
