Speedy

"Hey, how fast can you get?! The Speedy power-up will increase your speed a lot and that's not only good to hunt your opponents with the Punchy, it also helps you escape." — World of Padman Manual
The Speedy is a powerup that adds 2x bonus to your running speed. It lasts for 30 seconds and makes small cloud trails emerge form behind your feet as long as you have it. It respawns after two minutes. Upon contact with a teammate or enemy, you will push him/her away! However no damage is dealt to them, but it can be useful for enemies to miss their target. Keep in mind however, that your trails make enemies easier to track you. Unlike its Quake 3 Arena counterpart, it does nothing to the rate of fire of the player's weapons.

Strategies

•If you are up against someone with Speedy, prepare something with high damage or rate of fire to intercept the user. Looking for trails will make finding the user easier. Avoid fighting Speedy users at close range if your reflexes cannot cope with the enemy's speed or your ping is high. In addition, avoid contact with enemies that have Speedy as they will push you away!
•Look for predictable points, like the exit of a teleporter, choke points and narrow corridors that are difficult to maneuver. Ambushing Speedy users at these locations is often easier than unloading your weapon to them while they are on the run.
