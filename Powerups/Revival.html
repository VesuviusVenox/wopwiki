Revival

"You'll not be totally un-killable, but your health will regenerate automatically. With this item you'll get up to 200% health." — World of Padman Manual
The Revival, also known as REViVAL, is a powerup that regenerates the player's health for 30 seconds or until it hits 200 health. Depending on the map, it can last anywhere between 30 and 60 seconds, respawns after 2 minutes and makes heart trails when the user is moving. If the possessor has less than 100 health, Revival will heal 15 health every second. When the user's health reaches 100 the regeneration rate will drop to 5 health per second. The player can gain up to 200 points of health and it will not drop until Revival wears off.

Strategies

•Revival is a lifesaver, so try and grab it as soon as the match starts! A player that has Revival and a powerful weapon will be able to last for a while. However, don't think it makes you invincible, because a few quick and powerful shots in a row can still tear you up, even if you have it.
•If you see somebody with Revival, grab a powerful weapon and begin unloading it on the target as fast as you can. Revival can't catch up to large amounts of damage done quickly. If you are fighting without powerful weapons, do not try to fight the user and instead obtain any supplies you can get, especially health and armor. Try to drain the resources for the enemy because he has the advantage over you already.
•Revival bearers can shrug off low damage hits like pellets. If you're inaccurate, use faster weapons like the Bubble G. or get closer to deal more damage.
