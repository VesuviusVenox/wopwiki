Health Station

Instead of health pickups, WoP uses health stations. In order to recover one's health, a player must find a health station and stand in it until they are back to full health. Health Stations can only heal up to 100 health and won't go above. Each health station has a limited use, and it can heal around 300 health at a time. Once the cross in the circle is gone, the health station won't work for a minute until it has recharged and is ready for use again.
