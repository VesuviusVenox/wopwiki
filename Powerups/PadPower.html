PadPower

"This gives a mixture of power and protection. Picking up this power-up means that you need to hurry up with your fragging. You will do more than 2 times the normal damage and you will also protected by damage from your opponents! And as an extra, you own weapons don't hurt you either (try jumps with the Imperius or Betty)." — World of Padman Manual
PadPower is a powerup mixed with damage and protection. It amplifies a weapon's damage by a factor of 2, absorbs half damage from direct hits, grants full protection against splash and self damage, and allows the bearer to enter lava and slime without being harmed for 30 seconds.

Strategies

•With PadPower, you are a killing machine! Since splash damage doesn't affect you, use Betty/Balloony jumps to move around. You can also get close and personal with the Betty without worrying about splash damage, so don't worry if somebody suddenly appears in front of you. Just remember that opponents' weapons can still harm you, so don't think you're invincible.
•Direct hits from explosive weapons can still harm PadPower users, but the PadPower user can spam explosives in tight quarters.
•If you see somebody with PadPower, take out a powerful and quick projectile weapon, such as the Bubble G. or Splasher and use it against him. His suit only gives him little protection against non-splash damage and hitscan damages. Try to predict his movements (if possible), then blow him away.
•The PadPower will also keep the player from drowning. PadPower does NOT protect you from the void.

Trivia

PadPower's damage amplitude is controlled by the g_quadfactor console command. The default is 2, this means weapons will deal 2x damage and vice versa.
